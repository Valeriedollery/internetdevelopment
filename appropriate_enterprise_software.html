Appropriate Enterprise Software

Software used to meet a range of business requirements.

CRM: Customer Relations Management used to manage a company’s connection with its current and potential customers. It simply improves relationships to grow your business, and helps keep the company’s connection with customers, it also helps to improve profitability and make processes more efficient
Since moving to the cloud, systems such as Salesforce allow every user access to the same information. The people out on the road can update and check information instantly from any location.

Online Payment Systems: Businesses rely on payment to operate. It is essential the customer pays on time and in full to ensure the business can keep up with the cost of operating. The business should add as many options for payment as possible. It is suggested to use credit cards which are the most popular way to pay,  an Internet merchant account is needed which can be obtained in your local bank but to cover all major credit cards you could possibly need to use more than one bank which will require a security deposit and also take time

Supply Chain Management (SCM) is the management of goods and services to and from a business, it includes all processes involved in taking raw material to final products. The most important phases are planning, sourcing, production, distribution and returns. This is to control and reduce costs and avoiding supply shortages.  The two main types of software are planning applications to determine the best way to fill an order and execution applications to track the physical status of goods, management of materials and financial information.
Examples:
Global Supply Chain Management & International Logistic Services (shippabo.com)
Transportation Management System | FreightPOP  
Warehouse Software Built for High Volume Fulfillment | Logiwa 

Content Management Systems (CMS) is a computer software used to manage the creation and modification of digital content. It is used for enterprise content management (ECM) and web content management (WCM). This software helps create, modify and manage content without having to know how to write code.
